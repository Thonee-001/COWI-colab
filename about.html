Who We Are

Child of Wealth Initiative is a non-profit organisation born out of deep concern for children deprived of life’s basic needs, especially quality education, who are then left to struggle without hope and risk becoming a menace to society.

We are committed to empowering young minds in rural and semi-urban communities, restoring their dreams, and opening doors to a brighter future where every child can thrive.

What We Stand For

Our mission is to reach as many young ones as possible through initiatives such as:

  • Community and school outreaches
  • Sensitisation and awareness campaigns
  • Skill Acquisition - vocational training and tech empowerment
  • Educational Scholarships

We believe, as Marian Wright Edelman rightly said, “Children who are victims of poverty—educationally and otherwise—are the greatest threat to the well-being of a nation.”

We envision a world where no child is left on the streets, where every young mind is equipped with the tools to thrive, and where today’s children grow into visionary leaders who will stand out and transform their communities, the nation, and the world at large.
